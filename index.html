Anaglyph method comparison. A new twist: anaglyphs on the fly

The main purpose of this web app is to generate several anaglyphs following the methods exposed by Peter Wimmer on his know-how article Anaglyph Methods Comparison.

Anaglyphs are built on the fly using SVG filters.

A stereoscopic pair by Pascal Martiné —I still have to ask for his consent— is proposed in case you don't have any on hand.
